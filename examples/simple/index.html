Help

From a file explorer, drag Gocad files that contain points, lines or surfaces onto the drop panel. Gocad files of type points, lines or surfaces usually have the file extension of vs, pl and ts respectively.

Navgation Keys
  • A - Rotate anticlockwise
  • D - Rotate clockwise
  • W - Zoom in (also left mouse button)
  • S - Zoom out (also right mouse button)
  • R - Tilt towards
  • F - Tilt away
Warning We don't know what your environment is capable of. Very large files (>100 MB) may crash your browser. Some browsers and machines are capable of even larger (>200 MB) but that's an experiment for you.
